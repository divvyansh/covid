how to prevent virus

maintain distance

Everyone should understand the necessity of maintenance social distancing for sake of better and safe world. Social distancing is mandatory for safeguard yourself from highly contaminated virus. So keep yourself safe from large gathering and try to maintain safe distance from unknown person.

don't touch face

To reduce the spread of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19), experts recommend that you not touch your face because the virus is transmitted through mucous membranes in the mouth, nose and eyes.

wash your hand

Washing your hands is the best way to stop germs from spreading. Think about all of the things that you touched today â€” from the telephone to the toilet. Maybe you blew your nose in a tissue and then went outside to dig around the dirt.

When dining, using your napkin properly is an important part of the experience. Not only is it handy for blotting spills and patting your mouth, but it is also essential when you need to clean your hands. Here are some etiquette tips for using your napkin while eating.

wear a mask

Masking not only prevents respiratory droplet spread from asymptomatic infected persons to susceptible individuals while talking, coughing or sneezing but also acts as a barrier from face touching.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Risk of people spreading SARS-CoV-2 to animals. People can spread SARS-CoV-2 to animals, especially during close contact. Reports of animals infected with SARS-CoV-2 have been documented around the world

avoid close contact with peoples

Remember that some people without symptoms may be able to spread virus. Stay at least 6 feet (about 2 arm lengths) from other people. Keeping distance from others is especially important for people who are at higher risk of getting very sick.!

avoid crowded area

The risks of getting COVID-19 are higher in crowded and inadequately ventilated spaces where infected people spend long periods of time together in close proximity. These environments are where the virus appears to spread by respiratory droplets or aerosols more efficiently, so taking precautions is even more important.
